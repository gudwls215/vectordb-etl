1. EPS TOPIK and language course

  1. Academic scholarship

    • Those who have excellent results in the previous curriculum (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

  2. Scholarship to a cooperative organization

    • Persons recommended by universities or other cooperative organizations (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

2. Certification course

  1. Academic scholarship

    • Those who have excellent results in the previous curriculum (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

  2. Scholarship to a cooperative organization

    • Persons recommended by universities or other cooperative organizations (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

  3. Career scholarship to industry

    • Persons with work experience in related industries (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

3. Person who has completed a duplicate course

  1. Scholarship for failed students

    • Those who repeat the previous curriculum (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees

  2. A multi-course student

    • Those who take language and beauty courses in duplicate (excluding internal scholarship regulations): 10-50% differential payment of tuition fees
