Course InformationCourse of enrollment

For those who visit for the first time, we will inform you of the course registration process easily.
If you have any more questions, please contact the Learning Support Center

1. Join membership

To register for the course, you must click on the membership registration at the top right of the website and agree to the terms and conditions.
After agreeing to the terms and conditions, enter your membership information to complete your membership registration.

Register

2. Login

Log in by entering the ID and password generated when signing up for membership.

  • You must enter my classroom to learn after registering for classes, and when you enter my classroom, you can log in in two ways: normal login and public certificate login.

3. Application for classes

You can check the list of courses you can register for on the course registration menu.
Please check the lecture contents and lecture preview of the subject guided through the learning design and apply for the class by clicking the class registration button.

Apply

4. Payment of tuition fees

The course registration process will be completed if you pay the tuition fee for the course you applied for.
The tuition fee can be paid (paid) by choosing one of the payment methods such as cart, real-time account transfer, online bankbook deposit (virtual account), and simple payment

5. Course approval

After completing the payment, you can check the course information of the course I applied for in the course registration status of the course you paid for in [My Classroom]-[ Course History].
Among the payment methods, if you pay using credit card payment, you will be approved immediately, but if it is an online bankbook deposit (virtual account), learning will take place from the start of the semester after the administrator approves it.

6. Start of class

Learners who have completed the payment stage can learn the subject they applied for from the start of the semester.
Please check whether your membership information is properly registered and make sure to check the notice every time you access it.

7. Learning

You can learn in my classroom.
To learn in my classroom, you can enter in the following way.

  • on the upper right Click the mark on the upper right to log in, [My Class] - [Click subject name]

My class

8. Finishing course

The study period may vary from subject to subject, but the subject ends at the end of the set time.
The final score can be checked during the period set by the headquarters after the final exam.

9. Completion of a course

If you meet all the conditions for completion, you can check your grades and attendance after the end of the semester.
If you complete the set content of learning, you can complete the course and get a certificate of completion of the course.
