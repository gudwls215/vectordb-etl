Course InformationCriteria for return of learning expenses

■ Enforcement Decree of the Lifelong Education Act.(revision 2019.7.2)
Classification Date of occurrence of reason for return Amount of return
1. In the case of reasons for return under Article 28 (4) 1 and 2 of the Act the day when a class cannot be held or a class place cannot be provided Amount calculated on a daily basis by the amount of study expenses already paid
2. In the case of reasons for return under Article 28 (4) 3 of the Act A. In the case of the collection period for learning expenses is within one month 1) Before the class begins Full amount of study expenses already paid
2) Before 1/3 of the total class time has passed Two thirds of the learning expenses already paid
3) After one-third of the total class time, until one-half of the class time has passed Amount equal to 1/2 of the learning expenses already paid
4) After 1/2 of the total class Do not return
B. In the case of the period of collection of learning expenses exceeds 1 month 1) Before the class begins Full amount of study expenses already paid
2) After the start of the class The sum of the returnable learning expenses for the month in which the reason for return occurred and the total amount of learning expenses for the rest of the month

Remarks

  1. The term "collection period of learning expenses" means the total number of class days according to the total amount of learning expenses collected.

  2. The term "total class hours" means the total class hours during the collection period of learning expenses.

  3. The calculation of the amount of return shall be based on the class hours elapsed until the date on which the reason for return occurs.

  • ※ Criteria for return of learning expenses: Based on Article 23 of the Enforcement Decree of the Lifelong Education Act [Full Story Amendment on August 2, 2016]
  • ※ Learning expenses refer to the amount excluding teaching materials and training expenses.
  • ※ Refund policy coverage may vary from course to course
  • ※ If the beneficiary of the scholarship requests a refund, it is paid based on the reduced tuition fee.
  • ※ If a refund is received at the request of the person, the card fee will be deducted and paid in accordance with the relevant regulations.
  • ※ The refund processing time will take about 5 days. Please understand.
