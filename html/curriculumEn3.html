Benefits and Necessity of the Cosmetic Bachelor's Degree Transfer Course

For beauty professionals who graduate from a two- to three-year junior college in the beauty field in Vietnam, or run a shop in person in the field, we introduce the benefits and necessity of the Korean Cyber University degree transfer course.

  1. Systematic and innovative beauty curriculum

    • Korea is recognized as a leading country in the beauty industry worldwide, and at the center of it is systematic and innovative beauty education. Korea Open Cyber University's degree program offers opportunities to learn these latest trends and advanced technologies remotely, and helps Vietnamese beauty professionals become competitive at the forefront of the global beauty market.

  2. Career development and expertise enhancement

    • For those with a career in the beauty field, this degree course offers an ideal foothold to move to a broader market. The advanced skills and knowledge you have learned through this course will further enhance your expertise and expand your opportunities to serve a wider customer base.

  3. The flexibility of on-line learning

    • For those with jobs, studying and working at the same time is a huge challenge. The remote education system at the Korea Open Cyber University allows users to access anytime, anywhere, and offers the flexibility to learn according to their schedules. It allows them to get degrees even in their busy daily lives, allowing them to continue their workplaces and learning at the same time. Being able to study for a K-Beauty degree in Vietnam is a supreme privilege.

  4. Opportunities for international networking

    • Through this process, you can build a network with beauty professionals not only in Vietnam, but also in Korea and other countries. International exchanges provide opportunities to encounter new ideas and technologies, and to broaden your horizons through cooperation with professionals from various cultural backgrounds.

  5. Benefits of long-term expertise and certificate retention

    • The knowledge and qualifications gained through a cosmetologist's degree will positively affect your professional career and help you secure a more stable job and a higher income in the long run. Also, an internationally recognized degree will allow you to compete in the global market as well.

  6. Establishing a foothold for Master's and Ph.D.s by going to graduate school

    • A bachelor's degree is to lay the groundwork for graduate school, where you study for master's and doctoral degrees. You will need to earn the degree you need to work as a professor on the university's pulpit in the future. It will be your first preparation for the profession and you will be qualified to train Vietnam's top beauticians.

This transfer course provides Vietnamese beauty professionals with the opportunity to adapt to the global market and unleash their skills on the world stage. Your future will be brighter through a professional curriculum and international experience.

Plan for the Operation of the Course for the Transfer of a Bachelor of Beauty Studies Degree

  1. Introducing a blended learning model

    • Real-time Interactive Classes and Self-directed Learning: Provide materials for students to learn autonomously by utilizing already established remote content, and regularly hold real-time web seminars and online discussion sessions. This encourages learners' participation and encourages interactive learning.

  2. Hands-on education

    • Practice Kit Offerings: Provide students with a home practice kit, so they can actually practice their makeup or hairstyling skills. We also develop modules that can be practiced in a virtual salon environment using VR technology.

    • Practice Video: Produce a practice video that introduces Korea's latest beauty technologies and trends so that students can watch and follow.

  3. Professional mentoring program

    • 1:1 Mentoring: Provide regular online mentoring sessions to each student by matching Korean beauty experts as mentors. This allows them to receive individual feedback and improve their expertise.

  4. Language and cultural education

    • Provide a variety of language options: Provide subtitles or voice dubbing for lecture materials and educational content in Korean as well as Vietnamese, so that you do not experience any inconvenience in learning due to language barriers.

    • Korean Culture Education: Include a curriculum on the Korean language and culture to help you understand the cultural elements that are behind the Korean beauty industry.

  5. Career development and employment support

    • Career Coaching: Provide individual career coaching to meet students' career goals.

    • Industry Linkage: Connect with the beauty industry in Korea and Vietnam to provide internships and job opportunities.

  6. Evaluation and certification

    • Portfolio Assessment: Have students submit their projects and lab results to their portfolios to evaluate their actual ability to work

    • International Certification: Provides graduation and employment opportunities. This course allows students to obtain internationally recognized certifications.

  7. Continuous education and community building

    • Regular workshops and seminars: Introduce the latest beauty trends and technologies through regular workshops and seminars for graduates.

    • Online Community: Provides an online platform for students and graduates to share their experiences and information, forming a network of experts and encouraging continuous learning.

This comprehensive curriculum operation plan will provide effective and systematic beauty education to Vietnamese students, which will greatly help them cultivate talented people with the professional skills and knowledge they need in the K-Beauty industry. The course will also provide various opportunities to support students to build successful careers in the global beauty industry.

K-Beauty Certification Course

  1. Basic Haircut Practice

    • This course introduces students to the basic skills of haircuts. Students learn how to handle tools such as scissors and clippers, Learn how to understand different hair types, learn basic cutting styles. The emphasis is on accuracy, safety, and hygiene. After the course, students will be able to perform basic hairstyles with confidence and knowledge.

    View sample lecture

  2. Basic Makeup Practice

    • In this course, students explore the essential principles of makeup application. Learning topics include skin preparation, color theory, and the use of various makeup tools. Students practice producing a variety of looks, from natural to dramatic. This process aims to develop techniques to improve facial features and apply makeup suitable for different situations.

    View sample lecture

  3. Basic Skin Care Practice

    • This course introduces essential skin care technologies and products. Students learn about different skin types and conditions, and how to create a customized skincare routine. It covers major practices such as cleansing, exfoliating, moisturizing, and using treatments. This course highlights the importance of maintaining healthy skin through proper care.

    View sample lecture

  4. Basic nail art practice

    • In this course, you will learn the basic skills of nail art. Topics include nail care, preparation, application of basic designs using polish and other materials. This course covers a variety of tools and techniques for creating simple yet sophisticated nail art. Eventually, students will be able to perform basic nail art services with confidence.

    View sample lecture

  5. introduction to cosmetology

    • This introductory course covers a wide range of cosmetology, including hair, skin, and nail care. Students gain a general understanding of the beauty industry, its history, and job opportunities. The curriculum includes basic skills and practice in various beauty fields. This course prepares students for more professional training and professional development.

    View sample lecture

  6. Disinfections and Epidemiology

    • This process focuses on the important aspects of hygiene and safety in the beauty industry. Learn various disinfection methods, the importance of disinfection, and the principles of epidemiology. This course emphasizes preventing the spread of infection and ensuring a safe environment for customers and professionals. The practical application of these methods in the salon environment will be a key component.

    View sample lecture

  7. Beauty management

    • This course introduces students to the business aspects of the beauty industry. Topics include salon management, customer service, marketing, and financial planning. Students learn how to create a successful beauty business, manage their staff, and maintain a high level of service. This course aims to equip students with the skills they need to effectively operate or manage their hair salons.
